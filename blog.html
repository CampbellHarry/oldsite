Scams

Posted by Harry Campbell

I don't usually talk about scams on LinkedIn however, About 2 days ago I saw one in action. Job seekers please read this to find the signs of these scams. First warning sign: WhatsApp? I don't know that many recruiters that reach out to me on WhatsApp if you have let me know. The first one that messaged me said she was Sophie though WhatsApp naming was different little things like that you should always check Second: Check the name of the so called company that they work for are they attempting to impersonate that company if you have applied for a job with your email address 9.5/10 they will send you a email from where you applied from. Third Check: the phone numbers. With mine I had an American new account with no profile picture and one English phone number with a older account. Forth Check: Domains. With this scam I saw that after doing a check it pointed to 3 different re directs all filled with great malware and other fun things. They were attempting to impersonate a company that had a .org domain they had a .com one. Keep safe and best of luck out there!
